About Us

Ambo University is a university in Ambo, Oromia, Ethiopia. It is approximately 119 kilometres west of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The Ministry of Education admits qualified students to Ambo University based on their score on the Ethiopian Higher Education Entrance Examination.
